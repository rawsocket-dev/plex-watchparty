Hand-off control — options to replace the dropdown

The current "Give Remote" is a native <select>, which looks like a stray form field. Four more deliberate treatments below — all do the same job (active host picks a connected user to hand control to). Pickers are shown open so you can see the full interaction.

1 · Pill button + popover (right side of strip)

A proper button ⇄ Give Remote ▾ on the right of the status strip; click opens a small panel of connected users with avatars. Scales cleanly to many viewers.

2 · The "driving" chip IS the control (recommended)

No separate widget. The chip that shows who's driving — DRIVING · Alice (you) ▾ — is itself clickable and opens the same picker. The place that shows control is the place you change it.
