Atrium

Glass panel floating above the screen, inset from the edges with a backdrop blur softening whatever's behind. Pill-shaped controls; the primary action glows ember. Warm rather than sci-fi — the same palette, just rendered as a halo instead of a hairline. Closest to what a modern set-top-box would ship.
